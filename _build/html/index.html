MXNet Documentation¶

MXNet is a deep learning framework designed for both efficiency and flexibility. It allows you to mix the flavours of deep learning programs together to maximize the efficiency and your productivity.

This section contains the design document and notes we made for mxnet system design and deep learning libraries in general. We believe that open sourcing the system design note can letgeneral audiences understand the motivations and the benefits drawbacks of design choices, and help those who uses deep learning and who builds deep learning systems.

This section will be updated with self-contained design notes on various aspect of deep learning systems, in terms of abstraction, optimization and trade-offs.
